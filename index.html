Welcome to DDD East Anglia!

DDD East Anglia is over for 2018

Thanks to all of the speakers, sponsors, helpers and attendees for their help and support in making DDD East Anglia a successful and enjoyable day. Photos from the event can be found on our Flickr page.

Slides and/or code for some of the talks can be found at the following links:

  • MediatR - bridging the gap between your SPA and your backend (Russell Seamer)
  • Functional C# (Simon Painter)

Reviews

You can read some write-ups of DDD East Anglia 2018 in the following blog posts:

  • Mark Clearwater - My first DDD Event and how I made the decision to work a 16 hour Saturday
  • Craig Phillips - DDD East Anglia 2018 in review

DDD East Anglia will be returning for another year. It will take place in Cambridge on Saturday 22 September 2018 at Hills Road Sixth Form College. Like all DDD community events, DDD East Anglia is free to attend, funded entirely through the generosity of our sponsors.
